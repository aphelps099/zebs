Discovery

YOUTUBE SEO

YouTube is a search engine. Optimize every video for search.

TITLE FORMULA

Include the search term, be specific, create curiosity.

  • "10-Minute Morning Mobility Routine for Seniors"
  • "Best Stretches for Lower Back Pain (50+)"
  • "Chair Exercises for Seniors — Full Body Workout"
  • "How to Improve Balance After 60"

KEYWORD TARGETS

Research these phrases — they're what your audience searches.

  • exercises for seniors
  • mobility exercises for elderly
  • 10 minute workout for over 50
  • stretches for back pain seniors
  • balance exercises for elderly
  • chair exercises for seniors

Visual

THUMBNAIL DESIGN

Thumbnails determine click-through. They matter as much as titles.

THUMBNAIL FORMULA

  • Zeb in action (or expressive face)
  • Bold text (3-5 words max)
  • High contrast colors
  • Consistent brand style
  • Easy to read at small size

AVOID

  • Cluttered designs
  • Small text
  • Stock photos
  • Clickbait that doesn't deliver
  • Inconsistent branding

Schedule

PUBLISHING RHYTHM

RECOMMENDED SCHEDULE

Frequency 1-2 videos per week
Best Days Tuesday and/or Thursday
Best Time 9:00 AM or 2:00 PM (local time)
Batch Filming Film 4-6 videos in one session

MONTH 1

Launch 10-Min Mobility series (7 videos). Establish presence.

MONTH 2-3

Add tutorial content. Film 10-Min Abs series.

MONTH 4+

Full content mix. 10-Min Booty series. Lifestyle content.

Launch

CHANNEL LAUNCH CHECKLIST

BEFORE LAUNCH

  • Create and brand channel
  • Write channel description
  • Create channel trailer (optional)
  • Design thumbnail template
  • Film first 5-7 videos
  • Write titles and descriptions
  • Create thumbnails

LAUNCH WEEK

  • Publish first video
  • Share on Facebook
  • Email list announcement
  • Publish second video (2-3 days later)
  • Respond to all comments
  • Monitor analytics
  • Adjust based on feedback
