Setup

CHANNEL OPTIMIZATION

Set up your channel for discoverability and trust. First impressions matter.

Channel Branding

  • Name: Zeb's Platinum Fitness
  • Handle: @zebsplatinumfitness
  • Profile: Same as Facebook (consistency)
  • Banner: Branded with "Fitness for 50+"
  • Watermark: Logo in corner of all videos

Channel Description

  • Hook: Personal training and fitness education for adults 50+
  • Credibility: I'm Zeb - I'm in my 60s and I train people every day
  • Promise: If you think it's too late to get fit, watch me
  • Content: 10-minute workouts, Mobility tips, No-nonsense advice
  • CTA: zebsplatinumfitness.com

YOUTUBE SEO

YouTube is a search engine. Optimize every video to be found by people actively looking for solutions.

T

Include the search term, be specific, create curiosity.

10-Minute Morning Mobility Routine for Seniors
Best Stretches for Lower Back Pain (50+)

Keyword Targets

Research these phrases - they're what your audience searches.

Visual

THUMBNAIL DESIGN

Thumbnails determine click-through rate. They matter as much as titles.

✓

Do This

  • Zeb in action (or expressive face)
  • Bold text (3-5 words max)
  • High contrast colors
  • Consistent brand style
  • Easy to read at small size
✗

Avoid

  • Cluttered designs
  • Small text
  • Stock photos
  • Clickbait that doesn't deliver
  • Inconsistent branding
