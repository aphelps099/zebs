Content Pillars

WHAT TO POST

EDUCATION (60%)

Teaching content that demonstrates expertise and provides immediate value.

  • Exercise tutorials
  • Mobility tips and quick fixes
  • Common mistake corrections
  • Nutrition basics for 50+
  • Supplement education
  • "Why this works" explanations
  • Myth busting

PROOF (20%)

Evidence that the system works — builds credibility and trust.

  • Client transformations
  • Testimonials (video preferred)
  • Before/after stories
  • Zeb's own fitness at 60+
  • Client milestone celebrations
  • Progress snapshots
  • Success interviews

PERSONAL (20%)

Human connection — people buy from people they trust and like.

  • Behind the scenes
  • Daily routine glimpses
  • Philosophy and mindset
  • Personal struggles/comebacks
  • Hobbies and interests
  • Family moments
  • Community involvement

Platform Strategy

WHERE TO POST

Don't try to be everywhere. Master one platform, then expand.

FACEBOOK

The primary platform. 50+ audience lives here. Groups, lives, and community building.

  • 3x posts per week minimum
  • Weekly live video
  • Build community group
  • Respond to all comments

Measurement

SUCCESS METRICS

What to track and what to ignore.

TRACK THIS

  • Email list growth (goal: 50/month)
  • Engagement rate (not just follower count)
  • Comments and messages received
  • Website traffic from social
  • Leads generated (inquiries, sign-ups)
  • Content that gets shared

IGNORE THIS

  • Vanity follower counts
  • Likes without engagement
  • Comparison to fitness influencers
  • Viral content pressure
  • Platform algorithm anxiety
  • Posting frequency guilt
