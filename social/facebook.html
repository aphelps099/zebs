Setup

PAGE OPTIMIZATION

PROFILE ELEMENTS

  • Profile Photo: Zeb's headshot (clear, friendly, professional)
  • Cover Photo: Branded image with tagline
  • About: Clear description with "50+" and "Redding, CA"
  • CTA Button: "Book Now" or "Send Message"
  • Contact info: Phone, email, website
  • Hours: Training availability

  • Enable messaging

  • Add service offerings

  • Set up appointments

Content

POST TYPES THAT WORK

High Engagement

VIDEO POSTS

Native video gets 10x the reach of links. Always upload directly.

  • Exercise demonstrations
  • Quick tips (under 60 sec)
  • Client testimonials
  • Behind the scenes

High Engagement

FACEBOOK LIVE

Live video gets priority in the feed. Schedule weekly.

  • Weekly Q&A sessions
  • Live workouts
  • Topic deep-dives
  • Client spotlights

Steady Performer

IMAGE + TEXT

Photos with personal stories or tips. Keep text conversational.

  • Transformation photos
  • Quote graphics
  • Workout recaps
  • Milestone celebrations

AVOID THESE

Link posts (low reach), stock photos (impersonal), long text-only posts (low engagement), over-produced content (doesn't match audience expectations).

Weekdays: 9-11 AM and 1-3 PM (after morning routines, before afternoon activities)
Weekends: 9-11 AM (morning coffee time)
Avoid: Late evenings and early mornings

Community

FACEBOOK GROUP STRATEGY

A private group creates community and becomes a valuable asset for launches and loyalty.

GROUP SETUP

  • Name: "Zeb's Fitness Community — 50+ Strong"
  • Privacy: Private (request to join)
  • Description: Clear benefits + who it's for
  • Rules: Supportive, no spam, no medical advice
  • Questions: Email address (builds list)

GROUP CONTENT

  • Daily: Respond to all comments
  • Weekly: Exclusive tip or workout
  • Weekly: "Win of the Week" celebration
  • Monthly: Challenge or theme
  • Launches: Early access announcements

Templates

POST FORMULAS

Education Post

Hook: [Attention-grabbing question or statement]

[2-3 sentences of context/problem]

The tip: [Clear, actionable advice]

[Why it works]

CTA: Try this today and let me know how it goes!

Testimonial Post

Intro: Meet [Name]...

[Their starting point/challenge]

Transformation: [What changed]

[Quote from client]

CTA: Your transformation is waiting. DM me to get started.

Personal/Mindset Post

Hook: [Vulnerable opening or question]

[Personal story or reflection]

Lesson: [What I learned]

CTA: What's your take? Drop a comment below.

Engagement Post

Question: [Simple, easy-to-answer question]

[Brief context if needed]

Examples:

• What's your biggest fitness struggle right now?
• Morning workout or evening — which team are you?
• What would you do with an extra 10 minutes of energy each day?

Engagement

BUILDING RELATIONSHIPS

DO THIS DAILY

  • Respond to every comment (within 2 hours)
  • Reply with questions to encourage conversation
  • Like and comment on followers' posts
  • Answer all DMs personally
  • Thank people for sharing

AVOID

  • Generic "Thanks!" replies
  • Ignoring negative comments
  • Auto-responses only
  • Pitching in every reply
  • Deleting constructive criticism
