5 exercises I'd NEVER do after 50: (Unpopular opinion. But hear me out.) 1. Behind-the-Neck Press ❌ Destroys rotator cuffs ❌ Zero benefit over front press ✅ Do this instead: Standard overhead press 2. Upright Rows ❌ Impinges shoulder joint ❌ High injury risk ✅ Do this instead: Face pulls or lateral raises 3. Heavy Barbell Back Squats ❌ Compresses spine ❌ Requires perfect mobility most don't have ✅ Do this instead: Goblet squats, leg press 4. Kipping Pull-ups ❌ Joint destroying nonsense ❌ Momentum, not muscle ✅ Do this instead: Strict pull-ups or lat pulldowns 5. Box Jumps for Reps ❌ Achilles injuries waiting to happen ❌ Fall risk is real ✅ Do this instead: Step-ups with weight I've trained 1000+ clients over 50. These exercises hurt more people than they help. Train smart. Train long.
