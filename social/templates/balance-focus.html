Here's a stat that should get your attention: Balance declines about 3% per year after age 50. That might not sound like much - until you realize that 1 in 4 adults over 65 will fall this year. And 95% of hip fractures come from falls. The good news? Balance is trainable at any age. Simple exercises you can start today: - Stand on one foot while brushing teeth - Walk heel-to-toe in a straight line - Practice sitting and standing without using hands These small habits compound.
