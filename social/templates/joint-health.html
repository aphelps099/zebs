JOINT HEALTH 101 Worried about your knees? Hips? Shoulders? Here are 4 simple ways to protect your joints: 1. STAY HYDRATED Cartilage is 80% water. Dehydration = stiffer joints. 2. MOVE DAILY "Motion is lotion." Gentle movement keeps joints lubricated and healthy. 3. BUILD MUSCLE Strong muscles around a joint = less stress on the joint itself. 4. MAINTAIN HEALTHY WEIGHT Every pound of body weight = 4 pounds of pressure on your knees. The mistake most people make? Avoiding movement because of joint pain.
