Chair Squats

Target: Legs & Core

Suggested Caption

EXERCISE SPOTLIGHT: Chair Squats One of the most underrated exercises for building functional strength - especially if you're just getting started. WHY I LOVE THIS EXERCISE: - Safe for beginners - Builds the leg strength you need for daily life - Easy to progress as you get stronger HOW TO DO IT: 1. Stand in front of a sturdy chair 2. Feet shoulder-width apart 3. Lower yourself slowly until you touch the chair 4. Stand back up using your legs (not momentum) Start with 10 reps. Rest. Repeat 3 times. The goal isn't to sit - it's to control the descent. That's where the strength comes from.
