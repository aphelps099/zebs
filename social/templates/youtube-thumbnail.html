YouTube Thumbnail Best Practices

  • Use large, bold text - must be readable at small sizes
  • Include a face or person - thumbnails with faces get 38% more clicks
  • Use contrasting colors - yellow on black stands out in feeds
  • Keep it simple - 3 elements max (title, face, brand element)
  • Be consistent - use the same layout across a series
  • Show the benefit - "10-Minute" communicates quick value
  • Avoid text in the bottom-right corner (duration badge covers it)
