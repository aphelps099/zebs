ASK ZEB: "Is it too late to start at 60?" I get this question all the time. Here's my honest answer: No. Absolutely not. The key is starting with the RIGHT program: - Proper progression (start light, build slowly) - Focus on form over weight - Exercises appropriate for your current fitness level - Consistency over intensity You don't need to have exercised before.
