5-Minute Morning Routine

  • 01 Deep Breaths 30 sec
  • 02 Neck Circles 1 min
  • 03 Shoulder Rolls 1 min
  • 04 Standing Stretch 1.5 min
  • 05 March in Place 1 min

Total: 5 Minutes

Suggested Caption

Your morning doesn't need an hour-long workout. It needs 5 intentional minutes. Here's my go-to morning routine that takes less time than making coffee: 1. Deep breaths (30 sec) - Wake up your nervous system 2. Neck circles (1 min) - Release overnight tension 3. Shoulder rolls (1 min) - Open up your upper body 4. Standing stretch (1.5 min) - Lengthen your spine 5. March in place (1 min) - Get blood flowing Do this every morning for a week. You'll feel the difference by day 3.
