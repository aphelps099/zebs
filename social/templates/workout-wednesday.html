WORKOUT WEDNESDAY: Full Body Strength No gym. No equipment. Just 15 minutes. Here's a complete strength workout you can do at home: CIRCUIT (repeat 2-3 rounds): - Wall Push-ups: 12 reps - Chair Squats: 10 reps - Standing Rows (with band or towel): 12 each arm - Marching in Place: 1 minute - Bird Dogs: 8 each side - Calf Raises: 15 reps Rest 30-60 seconds between rounds. The key: Move slowly and controlled. Quality over speed. Try this today and let me know how it goes!
