Protein at Every Meal

The single most important nutrition habit for building strength after 50.

Suggested Caption

SIMPLE NUTRITION TIP #1: Protein at Every Meal If there's ONE nutrition change that matters most after 50, it's this. Here's why: After 50, your body becomes less efficient at using protein. You need MORE of it to maintain muscle - not less. The fix is simple: - Include protein at breakfast (eggs, Greek yogurt) - Include protein at lunch (chicken, fish, legumes) - Include protein at dinner (lean meat, tofu, beans) Aim for about 25-30g per meal. That's it. No fancy diet required. This single habit will do more for your strength than any supplement ever could. What's your go-to protein source?
