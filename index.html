THE BRAND

Positioning Statement

For adults 50+ who feel like fitness has passed them by, Zeb's Platinum Fitness is the personal training studio that delivers simple, effective programs with unwavering accountability. Unlike generic gyms that ignore older adults or high-intensity programs designed for 25-year-olds, Zeb provides personalized coaching built on decades of experience and proof that it's never too late to transform.
