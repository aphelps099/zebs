The Dan Go Model

The 10-Minute Series is a lead magnet, not a giveaway. Users provide their email to access the content. This builds the email list, which is where the real business happens. Social content drives traffic to lead magnets, email nurtures trust, paid products generate revenue. The free content is the hook - not the whole fishing rod.
