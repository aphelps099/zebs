GOING ONLINE

Month-by-month guide to building Zeb's digital presence from zero

The Mission

FROM LOCAL TO DIGITAL

Zeb has built a successful local training business. This playbook maps out the transition to an online presence — adding digital revenue streams while maintaining the local foundation.

01

START SIMPLE

Don't try to be everywhere. Master one platform, build one product, grow one list.

02

CONSISTENCY WINS

Better to post 3x/week for a year than 7x/week for a month then burn out.

03

VALUE FIRST

Build trust through free content. Selling comes naturally after trust is established.

Phase 1

MONTH 1: FOUNDATION

Build the infrastructure. Create assets. Establish baseline presence.

WEEK 1-2: SETUP

  • Optimize Facebook business page
  • Create YouTube channel (don't publish yet)
  • Set up email platform (ConvertKit, etc.)
  • Create lead magnet PDF
  • Build simple landing page for email capture

WEEK 3-4: CONTENT

  • Film 5-7 mobility videos (batch session)
  • Create thumbnails for all videos
  • Write welcome email sequence (4 emails)
  • Plan first month's content calendar
  • Test all systems (opt-in flow, emails)

Execution

SUSTAINABLE WEEKLY RHYTHM

A realistic schedule that maintains momentum without burnout.

Day Time Activity
Monday 30 min Post FB content + respond to weekend engagement
Tuesday 1 hour Film/edit YouTube video or batch content
Wednesday 30 min Post FB content + community engagement
Thursday 1 hour Write weekly email + schedule social
Friday 30 min Post FB content + prep for Saturday Live
Saturday 30 min FB Live or pre-recorded video release
Sunday — Rest / Plan next week's content

TOTAL: ~5 HOURS/WEEK

This is sustainable alongside a full training schedule. Batch filming on slower client days makes video production efficient.
