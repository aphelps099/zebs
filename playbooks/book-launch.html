Publishing

PUBLISHING PATHS

SELF-PUBLISHING

Maintain control, keep more profit, move faster.

Timeline 3-6 months from manuscript
Investment $2,000-5,000 (editing, design, etc.)
Royalty 35-70% of sale price
Control Full creative control

Platform: Amazon KDP, IngramSpark

TRADITIONAL PUBLISHING

Credibility, distribution, but less control and profit.

Timeline 12-24 months from deal
Investment $0 (publisher pays you)
Royalty 10-15% of sale price
Control Limited (publisher decides)

Requires: Agent + proposal + platform

RECOMMENDATION

Start with self-publishing. Build audience and proof of concept first. If the book performs well, you can approach traditional publishers with leverage for a second book or expanded edition.

Process

WRITING THE BOOK

Phase 1

OUTLINE

  • Finalize chapter structure
  • Create detailed outline
  • Gather stories/examples
  • ~2-4 weeks

Phase 2

DRAFT

  • Write first draft
  • 500-1000 words/day
  • Don't edit while writing
  • ~8-12 weeks

Phase 3

EDIT

  • Self-edit first pass
  • Beta readers feedback
  • Professional editor
  • ~4-6 weeks

Phase 4

PRODUCE

  • Cover design
  • Interior formatting
  • Upload to platforms
  • ~2-4 weeks

WRITING TIPS FOR NON-WRITERS

  • Talk it out: Record yourself explaining concepts, then transcribe
  • Use your content: Blog posts, emails, videos become chapters
  • Tell stories: Client examples make concepts real
  • Write like you talk: Your voice is your brand
  • Hire help: Ghostwriter or developmental editor can shape raw material

Launch

BOOK LAUNCH STRATEGY

PRE-LAUNCH (4-6 WEEKS)

  • Announce book to email list
  • Create pre-order bonuses
  • Recruit launch team (early readers)
  • Line up podcast appearances
  • Prepare launch week content
  • Set up Amazon author page

LAUNCH WEEK

  • Email sequence to list (5 emails)
  • Daily social media push
  • FB Live: Behind the book
  • Launch team reviews go live
  • Podcast episodes release
  • Track Amazon ranking

POST-LAUNCH

  • Continue podcast appearances
  • Local media outreach
  • Speaking opportunities
  • Book as lead magnet (first chapter)
  • Bundle with courses
  • Request ongoing reviews

BOOK FUNNEL

  • Entry: Buy book ($15-20)
  • Upsell 1: Video course ($47)
  • Upsell 2: Coaching ($197+)
  • Backend: Local training, supplements
  • Result: Book becomes client acquisition tool

Timeline

WHEN TO WRITE THE BOOK

PREREQUISITES BEFORE STARTING

The book will be more successful with these foundations in place:

  • Email list of 500+: Built-in launch audience
  • Consistent content creation: Raw material for chapters
  • Proven message: You know what resonates
  • Client success stories: Real examples to include
  • Complementary products: Upsells after book purchase

Realistic timeline: Begin book project around Month 6-12 of the "Going Online" roadmap. Focus on building audience first.
