SUPPLEMENT BUNDLES

How to build and sell supplement bundles without being salesy

Approach

THE TRUST-FIRST MODEL

Supplements can be a significant revenue stream, but only if done right. The approach: recommend only what you use, educate before selling, and never compromise trust for commission.

THE PLAYBOOK

  • Only recommend products you personally use
  • Lead with education, not sales
  • Create curated bundles for specific needs
  • Be transparent about affiliate relationships
  • Never make medical claims
  • Position as trusted curator, not salesperson

NEVER DO THIS

  • Push products aggressively in sessions
  • Recommend products for commission alone
  • Make health claims without evidence
  • Partner with low-quality brands
  • Overwhelm clients with options
  • Create urgency with fake scarcity

Products

THE BUNDLE SYSTEM

Pre-built bundles simplify the decision and increase average order value. Each bundle solves a specific problem.

Entry Point

The foundation for every adult 50+. Covers the basics most people are missing.

  • Vitamin D3: Bone health, immunity

Recommended for: ALL clients

Sales Moments

The best supplement conversations happen organically. Here's when to introduce them.

IN-PERSON SESSIONS

  • When client asks about nutrition → Daily Essentials
  • When client reports poor sleep → Rest & Recovery
  • Assessment reveals recovery issues → Active Support

DIGITAL PRODUCTS

  • 10-Min Mobility → Joint Support bundle
  • 10-Min Abs/Booty → Active Support bundle

Revenue

BUSINESS MODEL OPTIONS

OPTION A: AFFILIATE

Partner with quality brands for commission-based sales.

Investment $0
Commission 10-25% typically
Fulfillment Handled by brand
Best For Starting out, testing demand

Recommended partners: Amazon Associates, iHerb, Thorne, Pure Encapsulations affiliate programs

OPTION B: DIRECT SALES

Stock and sell supplements directly to clients.

Investment $500-2000 initial
Margin 40-60% typically
Fulfillment You handle inventory
Best For Established demand, local clients

Approach: Start with affiliate, then stock top sellers once you know what moves.

RECOMMENDED PATH

Phase 1: Start with affiliate links. Track which products get clicks and sales.
Phase 2: Once you identify top 3-5 products, consider stocking them for local clients.
Phase 3: Create "Zeb's Recommended" product page with affiliate links for online audience.

Script

SAMPLE CONVERSATION

How to introduce supplements naturally without being pushy.

Client: "My knees have been bothering me lately. Any suggestions besides the exercises?"

Zeb: "A few things have helped me and my clients. First, are you getting enough omega-3s? Most people aren't, and they're great for reducing inflammation. Second, I've seen good results with glucosamine for joint support — takes a few weeks to notice a difference. And collagen has helped some folks with cartilage health."

Client: "I don't even know where to start with that stuff."

Zeb: "I put together a simple bundle of what I recommend for joint health. If you want, I can send you the link — no pressure, just makes it easier than trying to figure out which brands are quality."

Compliance

STAY LEGAL

SAFE TO SAY

  • "This has helped me and some clients"
  • "Research suggests..."
  • "Many people find it helpful for..."
  • "I take this daily because..."
  • "Always check with your doctor first"

NEVER SAY

  • "This will cure your [condition]"
  • "You don't need your medication if..."
  • "This prevents [disease]"
  • "Everyone should take this"
  • "This is guaranteed to work"

IMPORTANT

You are not a doctor. Always recommend clients discuss supplements with their healthcare provider, especially if they take medications. Disclosure: clearly state when you have affiliate relationships.
