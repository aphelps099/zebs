The Strategy

Sell First. Build Later.

You don't have an audience yet. Giving away free content builds a list of freebie-seekers who rarely convert. Instead: sell the 10-minute series at a low price point. This does three things:

1. Validates Demand

If people won't pay $19, they won't pay $47 later. A sale proves the concept works.

2. Builds Buyers

A customer list is worth 10x a freebie list. Buyers buy again. Freebie hunters don't.

3. Generates Revenue

Cash from day one. 50 sales at $19 = $950. Real money, not hypothetical upsells.

The Math

50 buyers at $19 is better than 500 email subscribers who never pay. Quality over quantity.

Pricing

Simple. Low Risk.

Start with a single product at a no-brainer price. Add bundles later once you have buyers.

Sales Page

Copy Structure

Simple, direct, no fluff. Every section earns its place.

Section 1

Headline

"Move Better in 10 Minutes a Day"

A simple mobility routine from a 70+ trainer who rebuilt his body after back surgery.

Section 2

The Problem

You wake up stiff. Getting off the floor takes effort. You've tried stretching but nothing sticks. You're not injured — you just don't move like you used to.

Section 3

The Solution

10 minutes. 6 movements. No equipment. Do it with me every morning. Designed for adults 50+ who want to move without stiffness, pain, or limitation.

Video Structure

10-Minute Routine

Follow-along format. Zeb on camera. Simple setup at the studio.

0:00 – 0:45

Intro

"Hey, I'm Zeb. Welcome to 10-Minute Mobility." Quick credibility. Set expectations.

0:45 – 2:15

Neck & Upper Back

Chin tucks, neck circles, thoracic rotations

"This is where we store stress. Let it go."

2:15 – 3:45

Shoulders

Arm circles, wall slides, doorway stretches

"If you can't get your arms overhead without pain, this is for you."

3:45 – 5:15

Spine

Cat-cow, seated spinal twist, gentle side bends

"Your spine needs movement to stay healthy."

5:15 – 7:00

Hips

Hip circles, 90/90 stretch, pigeon variation

"Tight hips cause back pain. This undoes that."

7:00 – 8:30

Legs

Hamstring stretch, quad stretch, calf raises

"These muscles get short when we don't use them."

8:30 – 9:30

Integration

World's greatest stretch — puts it all together

"One movement that touches everything."

9:30 – 10:00

Outro

"That's 10 minutes. Do this every morning and you'll feel different in a week."

Soft CTA: "If you're in Redding, I train clients at Zeb's Platinum Fitness."
