Overview

Executive Summary

The 10-Minute Mobility Series is a lead generation funnel - not a free giveaway. Users provide their email to access the content. This first series serves as a template for future mini-courses (Booty, Abs, etc.).

Landing Page

Email-gated page with compelling copy, built in Squarespace. Users must enter their email to access the video content.

Core Video

10-minute mobility routine (gated content after email capture). The main product delivered after signup.

Promo Video

"Who Should Join" teaser video for social sharing (ungated). Drives traffic to the landing page.

Sharing Strategy

Distribution roadmap for launch and ongoing promotion across Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.

Section 1

Landing Page Structure

The landing page follows a proven lead-capture formula: hook, problem, solution, credibility, and call-to-action. Built for Squarespace with mobile-first design.

Hero Section

10 Minutes to Move Better

A free daily mobility routine from a 70+ year-old trainer who rebuilt his body after back surgery. No gym required.

CTA: Get Free Access →

Problem Section

"Sound familiar?"

  • You wake up stiff and it takes 20 minutes before you feel human
  • Getting up from the floor feels like a project
  • You've tried stretching but nothing sticks
  • You're not injured - you just don't move as well as you used to
  • You worry about what the next 10 years will feel like

Solution Section

"What if you could feel better in just 10 minutes a day?"

This isn't another stretching video you'll watch once and forget. It's a simple, repeatable routine designed specifically for adults 50+ who want to move without stiffness, pain, or limitation.

  • 10-Minute Follow-Along Video: Do it with me, every movement, every breath
  • No Equipment Needed: Just you, the floor, and 10 minutes
  • Designed for Real Bodies: Modifications shown for every movement
  • Instant Access: Enter your email and start today

"Why trust a 70-year-old trainer?"

Because I've lived it. After major back surgery, doctors weren't sure I'd train again. I rebuilt everything - mobility first, then strength, then confidence. Now I'm stronger at 70+ than most people half my age. Not because I'm special, but because I found what works. This routine is part of that system.

Section 2

Video Content

Length: 10-12 minutes | Format: Follow-along workout with Zeb on camera | Location: Zeb's Platinum Fitness studio | Hosting: Unlisted YouTube or Vimeo (link sent after email capture)

0:00 - 0:45

Intro

"Hey, I'm Zeb. Welcome to 10-Minute Mobility." Quick credibility: "I'm 70+ and I rebuilt my mobility after back surgery using routines like this." Set expectations: "We're going to move through 6 positions. Each one targets a common problem area. No equipment, no excuses."

0:45 - 2:15

Movement 1: Neck & Upper Back

Chin tucks, neck circles, thoracic rotations

"This is where we store stress. Let it go."

2:15 - 3:45

Movement 2: Shoulders

Arm circles, wall slides (or standing version), doorway stretches

"If you can't get your arms overhead without pain, this is for you."

3:45 - 5:15

Movement 3: Spine

Cat-cow, seated spinal twist, gentle side bends

"Your spine needs movement to stay healthy. This is lubrication."

5:15 - 7:00

Movement 4: Hips

Hip circles, 90/90 stretch (with modification), pigeon variation

"Tight hips cause back pain. Most people sit all day - this undoes that."

7:00 - 8:30

Movement 5: Legs

Hamstring stretch, quad stretch, calf raises

"These muscles get short when we don't use them. Let's wake them up."

8:30 - 9:30

Movement 6: Integration

World's greatest stretch, or a simple flow combining multiple movements

"This puts it all together. One movement that touches everything."

9:30 - 10:00

Outro

"That's 10 minutes. Do this every morning - or every other morning - and you'll feel different in a week." Soft CTA: "If you're in Redding and want more structure, I train clients just like you at Zeb's Platinum Fitness. Link in the description." "Now get moving."

Section 3

"Who Should Join" Promo Video

Length: 60-90 seconds | Purpose: Social sharing, Facebook/Instagram ads, drive traffic to landing page | Tone: Direct, relatable, no hard sell

Script

[Opening - 10 sec]
"This video is for you if you wake up stiff... if getting off the floor takes planning... or if you just don't move as well as you used to."

[The Problem - 20 sec]
"Here's the thing - most people over 50 don't have a mobility problem. They have a consistency problem. You've tried stretching. It didn't stick. Not because stretching doesn't work, but because you didn't have a simple routine you could do every day."

[The Solution - 20 sec]
"I put together a 10-minute routine you can do first thing in the morning. No equipment. No gym. Just you and the floor. I'm 70+ and I use this exact sequence to stay moving."

[Credibility - 15 sec]
"After my back surgery, I wasn't sure I'd train again. This routine is part of what got me back. If it worked for me, it'll work for you."

[CTA - 15 sec]
"Get the free video at [URL]. Enter your email, and you'll have instant access. Ten minutes. That's all it takes. See you there."
