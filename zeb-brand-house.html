Going Online

You're starting from zero. No social media. No YouTube. That's fine. We build it step by step. The goal isn't to become an influencer — it's to build a machine that generates leads and revenue while you train clients.

The Plan

Month 1

Foundation

Launch 10-Minute Mobility. Landing page + gated video + email capture. Share with current clients. Ask them to share. Goal: 50 emails.

Month 2

Facebook

Start posting 3x/week. Short clips from the 10-minute video. Your thoughts on fitness over 50. Client wins (with permission). No dancing. Just real.

Month 3

YouTube

Upload full 10-Minute Mobility to YouTube (public). Create 2-3 more videos: your story, common mistakes, what to expect. This is your library — it works forever.

Month 4-6

Expand

Launch 10-Minute Abs and 10-Minute Booty. Cross-promote. Email list grows. Consider first paid course ($47). Bundle supplements with courses.

Month 6+

Scale

Book launch. Online group coaching. Speaking opportunities. The flywheel is spinning — content builds audience, audience buys products, products fund more content.

The Commitment

2-3 hours per week. Film one video per month. Post 3x/week (can batch). Reply to comments. That's it. Consistency beats intensity.
