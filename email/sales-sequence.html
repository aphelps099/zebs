Create problem awareness and emotional connection. No pitch yet — just relate to their struggle and hint that help is coming.

Best Practices

LAUNCH TIPS

DO

  • Lead with value before asking for sale
  • Tell stories that create emotional connection
  • Use real testimonials from real people
  • Address objections directly
  • Create genuine urgency (if applicable)
  • Respect people who don't buy

DON'T

  • Use fake scarcity or countdown timers
  • Send more than 5-6 emails per launch
  • Make promises you can't keep
  • Shame people for not buying
  • Use aggressive, pushy language
  • Forget to follow up after purchase
