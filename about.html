Biography

The Full Story

Zeb began working out with weights and educating himself on health and fitness when he was just 12 years old. Those early seeds blossomed into a passion for fitness, and the fire to stay fit and healthy has continued with Zeb for more than three decades since.

Zeb's education, personal experience, and research as a personal trainer has fueled his desire to make a difference for as many people as possible. Over the years, Zeb has created a variety of programs to help people maintain a healthy and fit lifestyle - with a special focus on adults over 50 who are often told their best years are behind them.

Zeb has experienced the personal challenges of recovering from major back surgery and having to create a new 100% for himself to maintain the active and fit lifestyle he has always desired. Throughout it all, Zeb has continued to read and research the latest literature on health, fitness, and nutrition, to ensure the most effective training and diet for the changing needs of his clients.

What sets Zeb apart is his focus on the most difficult challenge for people: the mental game. When you talk with Zeb about your own personal training and fitness goals, you will immediately see the passion, experience, and knowledge that makes him so effective as a trainer. He doesn't just teach exercises - he transforms mindsets.

Zeb Young is a personal trainer and fitness expert based in Redding, California, specializing in training adults 50 and older. With over 30 years of experience and his own journey rebuilding his body after major back surgery, Zeb brings both expertise and lived experience to every client. His philosophy centers on three pillars: structure, motivation, and accountability. Zeb is the founder of Zeb's Platinum Fitness and creator of the 10-Minute Fitness Series.
