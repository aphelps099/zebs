Project Plan

10-Minute Mobility Series

Objective: Build a lead generation funnel using gated video content to capture emails, demonstrate Zeb's expertise, and convert viewers into training clients. This series serves as a reusable template for future 10-Minute courses (Booty, Abs, etc.).

Deliverables

1. Landing Page

Email-capture page built in Squarespace

  • Headline: "10 Minutes to Move Better"
  • Problem/solution copy targeting 50+ adults
  • Credibility section (Zeb's surgery recovery story)
  • Email form with first name + email

2. Core Video (Gated)

10-minute follow-along mobility routine

  • 6 movement blocks with coaching cues
  • Modifications shown for all levels
  • Filmed at Zeb's Platinum Fitness
  • Hosted on unlisted YouTube/Vimeo

3. Promo Video (Ungated)

60-90 second "Who Should Join" teaser

  • Hook: "This is for you if..."
  • Problem → Solution → Credibility → CTA
  • Optimized for Facebook/Instagram sharing
  • Drives traffic to landing page

4. Sharing Strategy

Launch roadmap + ongoing promotion plan

  • Week 1-2: Soft launch, community groups, referral partners
  • Ongoing: 2-3 social posts/week rotation
  • 4-email nurture sequence for sign-ups
  • Optional: Facebook ads ($5-10/day)
