SUPPLEMENTS

Marketing strategy for supplement sales and bundle offerings

Overview

THE OPPORTUNITY

Supplements represent a significant passive revenue stream. The key: position as trusted curation, not hard selling. Zeb recommends what he personally uses and what works for his demographic.

01

TRUST-BASED

Only recommend products Zeb personally uses or has vetted. Credibility is everything.

02

CURATED SIMPLICITY

Overwhelming options paralyze. Offer clear, limited bundles that solve specific problems.

03

EDUCATION FIRST

Teach why supplements matter before offering solutions. Value leads, sales follow.

Product Strategy

RECOMMENDED BUNDLES

Pre-assembled bundles for specific goals. Simplifies decision-making and increases average order value.

Foundation Bundle

DAILY ESSENTIALS

The baseline for adults 50+ — covers the gaps most people have.

  • Vitamin D3: Bone health, immune support
  • Omega-3: Heart and brain health
  • Magnesium: Muscle function, sleep
  • Multivitamin: Fill nutritional gaps

Target: All clients

Performance Bundle

ACTIVE SUPPORT

For clients who are training regularly and want enhanced recovery.

  • Protein Powder: Muscle maintenance
  • Creatine: Strength and cognitive function
  • Collagen: Joint and skin health
  • Electrolytes: Hydration and performance

Target: Active clients

Recovery Bundle

JOINT SUPPORT

Targeted support for clients dealing with joint pain or mobility issues.

  • Glucosamine: Joint lubrication
  • Turmeric/Curcumin: Inflammation
  • Collagen: Cartilage support
  • Omega-3: Anti-inflammatory

Target: Mobility program clients

Sleep Bundle

REST & RECOVERY

For clients struggling with sleep quality — foundational for recovery.

  • Magnesium: Relaxation and sleep
  • Ashwagandha: Stress reduction
  • L-Theanine: Calm without drowsiness
  • Melatonin: Sleep cycle support

Target: Stressed/sleep-deprived clients

Sales Strategy

HOW TO SELL

DO THIS

  • Mention supplements naturally during training
  • Share what you personally take and why
  • Educate through content (posts, videos)
  • Offer bundles at checkout (program purchase)
  • Create "Zeb's Stack" social content
  • Partner with quality brands only

AVOID THIS

  • Pushing products aggressively
  • Promising unrealistic results
  • Recommending products you don't use
  • Partnering with low-quality brands
  • Making medical claims
  • Overwhelming with too many options

Revenue

BUSINESS MODEL

OPTION A: DIRECT SALES

Stock and sell supplements directly to clients.

Pros Higher margins, immediate availability
Cons Inventory management, upfront cost
Margin 40-60%

OPTION B: AFFILIATE

Partner with quality brands for commission-based sales.

Pros No inventory, passive income
Cons Lower margins, less control
Margin 10-25%

RECOMMENDATION

Start with affiliate partnerships to test demand and build content. Transition to direct sales for top-selling products once you have proof of concept and understand your clients' preferences.
