10-MIN MOBILITY

The flagship program — Daily mobility routines to improve flexibility, reduce pain, and increase range of motion

Overview

THE PROGRAM

10-Min Mobility is Zeb's flagship digital product — a comprehensive mobility program designed specifically for adults 50 and older who want to move better, feel stronger, and maintain their independence.

Unlike generic stretching routines found on YouTube, this program is built on decades of experience training real people with real limitations. Every movement is purposeful, achievable, and progressively builds functional strength.

"The best workout is one you'll actually do. Ten minutes, every day. That's it."

Program Content

30-DAY STRUCTURE

WEEK 1: FOUNDATION

  • Day 1-2: Baseline assessment + gentle intro
  • Day 3-4: Hip mobility basics
  • Day 5-6: Spine and back mobility
  • Day 7: Active recovery + reflection

WEEK 2: UPPER BODY

  • Day 8-9: Shoulder mobility
  • Day 10-11: Neck and upper back
  • Day 12-13: Arms and wrists
  • Day 14: Full upper body flow

WEEK 3: LOWER BODY

  • Day 15-16: Knee mobility + strength
  • Day 17-18: Ankle and foot work
  • Day 19-20: Hip openers deep dive
  • Day 21: Full lower body flow

WEEK 4: INTEGRATION

  • Day 22-24: Full body flows
  • Day 25-27: Morning vs evening routines
  • Day 28-29: Challenge progressions
  • Day 30: Assessment + next steps
