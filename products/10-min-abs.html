10-MIN ABS

Core-focused workouts that strengthen without strain — designed for real bodies, not fitness models

Overview

THE PROGRAM

10-Min Abs is a strategic expansion from the flagship mobility program, targeting women 40+ who want to strengthen their core without the intimidation of traditional ab workouts.

This program acknowledges the realities of post-40 bodies — hormonal changes, back sensitivity, and time constraints — while delivering effective, progressive core training.

"Strong core. Strong life. No crunches required."
