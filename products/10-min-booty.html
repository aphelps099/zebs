Overview

THE PROGRAM

10-Min Booty completes the trifecta of the 10-Minute Series. This program focuses on glute activation, hip strength, and lower body power — essential for everything from climbing stairs to preventing falls.

Beyond aesthetics, strong glutes are the foundation of functional movement. This program teaches proper activation patterns that most people miss, leading to better posture, reduced back pain, and increased mobility.
